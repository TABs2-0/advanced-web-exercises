About

Hi, I'm Joël —
a builder who designs
with intent.

I work at the intersection of design and code. I don't just mock up screens — I understand how they get built, and I care deeply about the gap between a Figma frame and what ships.

Based in Cameroon, working globally. My products span civic tech, SaaS platforms, and mobile apps. I love the moment a messy user flow becomes calm and clear — then I write the code to prove it works.
