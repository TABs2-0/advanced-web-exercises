About

Hi, I'm Joël —
a builder who designs
with intent.

I work at the intersection of design and code. I don't just mock up screens — I understand how they get built, and I care deeply about the gap between a Figma frame and what ships.

Based in Cameroon, working globally. My products range from civic tech tools to SaaS platforms and mobile apps. I love the moment a messy user flow becomes something calm and clear — and then I write the code to prove it works.

Process

How a product takes shape

01

Discover the real problem

User interviews, competitive audit, and flow mapping to surface what's actually broken — not just what looks broken.

02

Structure before pixels

IA and wireframes that define information hierarchy. No color, no chrome — just logic and flow.

03

System thinking, not screen thinking

Components, tokens, variants. Design once, scale everywhere. The system is the product.

04

Ship with precision

Handoff specs, interaction notes, and often — direct frontend implementation. What ships is what was designed.
